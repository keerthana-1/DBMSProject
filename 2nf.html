Second Normal Form

Rule:

A relational schema R is in 2NF if every nonprime attribute A in R is fully functionally dependent on the primary key of R.

Prime attributes: pid
Non-prime attributes: name, age, gender, disease, weight, address, ph.no
{pid}->{name, age, gender, disease, weight, address, ph.no}
Fully functionally dependent . so relation is in 2NF.
